Human-AI Interaction Experiment Protocol Design and Execution

May 2023 - May 2024 • Human Factors • Python (PyQt/OpenCV) • MATLAB

Summary: This experiment aimed to examine how humans (e.g., astronauts) may assist an AI-based system acting as mission control during a simulated deep space habitat maintenance task. I designed the experiment protocol, organized the experiment layout, recruited and trained 30+ participants, conducted the experiment, and analyzed the collected data. For this project, I developed a linear regression model for subject selection, created training protocols, and implemented automated data collection methods using Python OpenCV library. Additionally, I utilized prompt engineering techniques to initialize a large language model (ChatGPT) to act as mission control. Finally, I utilized parametric and nonparametric statistics to show significant and non-significant relationships between the quantifiable data.

Example artifacts: protocol, training materials, data-collection tooling, analysis outputs.
